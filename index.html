Free Consultations

See if we're a fit for you

How do I know if this program fits me?

We offer free 15 minutes consultations with each tutor. Schedule a consultation with one of our tutors, and we'll walk you through our services and help you figure out if we're a good fit.

What if it's not a fit for me?

We don't ask for payment until after our first session together. We want to make sure you have a great experience first! If your experience was not up to your expectations, we will gladly work with you to resolve any concerns.
