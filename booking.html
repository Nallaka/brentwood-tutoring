Need Help?

How do I select my tutor?

After selecting a service, you will be asked to select a staff, please select the tutor that you would like to work with to view their availability.

How do I see the earliest availability?

If you are not working with a specific tutor, select all avaliable staff after selecting a service to view all openings with our tutors.

How does confirmation work?

After scheduling a session with our tutors, they will confirm their availability and promptly reach out by email with more information and the video conference links.

How will payment be completed?

After your first session, the tutor will reach out with their payment information. We want to make sure you have a great experience first!
